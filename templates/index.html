How it works

Monitor the Earth, One Region at a Time

Environmental Monitor / AI uses satellite imagery and artificial intelligence to detect and measure environmental change — from forest loss to urban sprawl — across the Philippines and beyond.

1. Choose a Country & Year

Select a country and pick a historical baseline year — as far back as 2013.

2. Draw on the Map

Use the polygon or rectangle tool to outline any region — a forest, a city, a coastline, or a farm.

3. Compare Satellite Images

A historical snapshot is retrieved and placed side-by-side with today's imagery for direct visual comparison.

4. Read the AI Analysis

Our AI model estimates forest coverage change, population shifts, and environmental degradation expressed as percentages and interactive charts.

🌱

Our Mission

Deforestation, urbanization, and climate change are quietly reshaping our landscapes. This tool was built to make environmental monitoring accessible to everyone — students, researchers, local governments, and concerned citizens alike. By combining freely available satellite data with AI analysis, we hope to turn passive observation into informed action.
